Brief Overview:



Lakshadweep Islands is a group of islands situated in the Arabian Sea, off the southwestern coast of India.

Formation and Coral Atolls:

The Lakshadweep archipelago is primarily composed of coral atolls, which are ring-shaped coral reefs enclosing lagoons.

Scenic Landscapes:

The islands feature coconut groves, and dense vegetation.

Coral Reefs:

Lakshadweep is home to some of the most vibrant and diverse coral reefs in the world.

Marine Life:

The rich marine ecosystem around Lakshadweep includes a variety of coral species.
